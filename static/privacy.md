**Effective date:** July 20, 2026

The DM Reference bot and DM Playground Activity ("the Service") are operated as open-source tools for the DreamMaker community.

## Data we collect

None. The Service does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

- Discord interactions (slash commands, button clicks) are processed in memory and discarded immediately.
- The DM Playground Activity runs entirely in your browser. Code you write and execute never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it to a Discord channel.
- When you use "Share to Discord," your code and output are sent to the bot, posted to the channel, and then discarded.
- OAuth2 tokens obtained during the share flow are used once to verify your identity and are not persisted.

## Third-party services

The Service interacts with the [Discord API](https://discord.com/developers/docs) to handle commands and post messages. Your use of Discord is governed by [Discord's Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy).

The DM Playground downloads BYOND binaries from [byond-builds.dm-lang.org](https://byond-builds.dm-lang.org) to run DreamMaker code in an emulated environment in your browser.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
